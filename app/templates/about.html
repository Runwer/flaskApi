About Us It's Nice to Meet You!

Fliqpick is a fun way to build your personal top watch list! Fliqpick will display some of the best movies in the world and let you pick your personal favorites from the selection. Your selection will be added to your own personal toplist, where you can see both the score calculated from your choices and the overall score for each movie given by other users.

Finally, you can share your top list on Facebook, giving your friends the opportunity to see your favorite movie selection!
